# Listing & Selling NFTs on AreaX NFT

Once you have a minted NFT or an NFT that you own you can visit the NFT's page to list it on the market. 

## Selling Your NFT

1. Open up your NFT's page
2. Click "Sell".

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-1.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

3. Set your price.
4. Choose how long you want to list your NFT for.
5. Click "Sell".

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-2.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

6. Initialize your wallet by confirming in your wallet pop up.
7. Approve the NFT by confirming in your wallet.
8. Then press confirm.

<img
  src={require('/static/img/areax-listing-3.png').default}
  alt="areaX"
  width="70%"
  height="automatic"
/>

If you've followed all of these steps then your NFT is now listed for sale!
